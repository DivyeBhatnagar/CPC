About CPC

Empowering the next generation of developers

Who We Are

Core Programming Club (CPC) is the official technical club of the School of Computer Science & Engineering at IILM University, Greater Noida.

We are a community of passionate developers, innovators, and tech enthusiasts dedicated to fostering a culture of learning, collaboration, and excellence in technology.

Through hands-on workshops, competitive programming, hackathons, and industry interactions, we prepare students for successful careers in the tech industry.

Our Mission

To create a vibrant ecosystem where students can explore, learn, and master cutting-edge technologies through practical experience, collaborative projects, and industry exposure, ultimately building industry-ready developers who can make meaningful contributions to the tech world.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the leading technical community in the region, known for producing exceptional developers who drive innovation, lead tech initiatives, and contribute to solving real-world problems through technology and creative thinking.
